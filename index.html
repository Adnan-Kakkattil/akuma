Our Philosophy

心

Mind (Shin)

Cultivate a mind that is calm in the face of adversity. Focus, clarity, and unwavering intent are the weapons of the true martial artist.

技

Technique (Gi)

Perfection is a journey, not a destination. Through repetition and precise mechanics, we turn the body into an efficient instrument.

体

Body (Tai)

Forge the body through rigorous conditioning. Strength, flexibility, and endurance are the foundations upon which technique is built.

"The ultimate aim of the art of karate lies not in victory or defeat, but in the perfection of the character of its participants." - Gichin Funakoshi

Dojo Kun (Oath)

  • We will train our hearts and bodies for a firm unshaking spirit.
  • We will pursue the true meaning of the martial way, so that in time our senses may be alert.
  • We will observe the rules of courtesy, respect our superiors, and refrain from violence.
  • We will follow our gods and buddhas, and never forget the true virtue of humility.
  • We will look upwards to wisdom and strength, not seeking other desires.
  • All our lives, through the discipline of karate, we will seek to fulfill the true meaning of the way.
